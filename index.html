WHAT IS HPC

High Perfomance Computing club (HPC) is one of the many clubs offered at the Queensland Academy for Science, Maths and Technology (QASMT). This club is for students who are interested in computers and computer coding. In this club, we embark on many different project to either help educate others about technology or help create an impact on the community. Currently we have 3 ongoing projects, The Weather Project (Year 10), Scracth Workshop (Year 11) and a Supercomputing Project for the Year 12.


WEATHER PROJECT

The Weather Project aims to provide data on the Weather at QASMT. Currently we are observing the weather of our recently built, open planned building (J Block) in our school.
